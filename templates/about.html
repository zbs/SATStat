About Charts Schools

Feature Concept

This web application aims to aid students in this endeavor by providing them with key statistical information of public schools in New York City – statistics that can help them understand factors within schools that potentially correlate with high SAT scores. We attempt to provide users with the ability to assess potential correlations between such factors and average SAT scores. In doing so, the feature aims to shed light on important criteria to consider when evaluating a school’s ability to prepare students for the SAT1.
